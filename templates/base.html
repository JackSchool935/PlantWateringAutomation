Welcome to Your Plant Monitoring Dashboard

Plants are essential for life on Earth. They produce oxygen, absorb carbon dioxide, and provide food, shelter, and habitat for countless species, including humans.

Proper care of your plants involves monitoring soil moisture, light levels, and temperature. Did you know that overwatering is one of the most common mistakes in plant care? Ensuring that your plants receive the right amount of water at the right time is key to keeping them healthy and thriving.

Use the "Pump Data" section to control and automate your irrigation system, ensuring your plants receive adequate water based on real-time data.



The goal of this app is for you plant to automatically being updated depending on your plant's status. When you plant needs water it will receive water.
